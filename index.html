Privacy Policy for DayKap AI

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

DayKap AI is a daily logging and AI-assisted reflection app that operates offline. All user data is stored locally on the device. The app does not collect or share any personal information.

2. Data Collection

  • Personal Information: No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.
  • User Logs: All notes or reflections remain on the user’s device.

3. AI Model Usage

The app downloads an AI model (Gemma) once during setup to enable offline AI summarization. No user data is transmitted online.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs.

5. Security

All data remains on the user’s device. Users are responsible for maintaining their device security.
